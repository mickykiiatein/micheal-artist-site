Mícheál Keating is a Limerick-based media artist working across sound, image, and text, to explore the politics of place and memory.

Raised on a dairy farm in West Limerick, he initially pursued music, releasing two critically acclaimed LPs with the art-rock band *Bleeding Heart Pigeons* (2008-2020), before establishing a media art practice.

Between 2022 and 2025 he developed *Live Landscapes/Tírdhreacha Beo*, a sustained, process-led engagement with rural landscapes supported by the Arts Council of Ireland and Limerick City and County Council, which resulted in a self-published book/album and a solo exhibition at Spacecraft, Limerick. 

As a musician, he currently performs with improvised noise collective *Péist*, post-rock band *The Low Field* and singer/songwriter *Laura Duff*. He is also active as a music producer, media art technician, and festival organiser with *Féile na Gréine*.
